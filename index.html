The Akan people are ethic group of West Africa,The largest group is found in both Ghana and the Ivory Coast and the one of the biggest in the region.Thegreater Akan people(ethick group) speak Kwa language. The people who became known as the Akan migrated from the Sahel to the forest region of coastal west Africa between the 11th to 13th Century.
